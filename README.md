# The Book of Claude Fables

An interactive digital storybook chronicling the wisdom of Anthropic's Claude models through the lens of classic fables.

Each chapter features a model as a character in the forest — from the early helpful fox to the final storyteller — teaching timeless lessons about intelligence, restraint, balance, and narrative.

The book culminates with **Claude Fable**, the latest model who weaves all prior lessons into enduring stories.

## Features
- Beautiful illuminated storybook design
- 8 interactive pages with custom SVG illustrations
- Click to reveal morals and collect wisdom
- Table of contents with direct navigation
- Persistent "Your Codex" of collected lessons
- Final interactive section: Ask Claude Fable to weave a new fable from a modern dilemma you provide

## Local Use
Simply open `index.html` in any browser.

Keyboard controls:
- Left/Right arrows → Turn pages
- On the final page, press `C` to focus the fable generator

## The Arc
The fables are sequential. Earlier models teach foundational lessons. Claude Fable (the latest) becomes the one who turns every lesson into a living story the forest will remember.

This is both a tribute to the evolution of the Claude family and a playful exploration of what it means for an AI to not just answer — but to teach.

Built as a self-contained experience. No backend. No accounts. Just the book.
